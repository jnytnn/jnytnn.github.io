Games

These are the games that I like to play.

The first shooting game I tried was Valorant. I first tried this during final exam season during Spring 2021 (do not recommend) at College Library and have been trapped in a vicious cycle ever since.

The second game I've tried playing is Terraria. This is a recent game that I've started because one of my friends likes to play it and it's more chill compared to Valorant. It's a pixelated 2D world where your character can craft, build, explore, or combat monsters!

I also like to play Codenames! It's hard to explain how to play, but you should definitely give it a try! It's available to play online if you don't have the actual board game :)
